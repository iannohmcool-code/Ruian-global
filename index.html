Graphic Design – From Zero to Paid

Start with Canva (free), design posters for local businesses, churches, events. Charge Ksh 500–2000 per design. Build a portfolio, then join Fiverr/Upwork.

7‑Day Action Plan: Day 1–3: Learn Canva basics (YouTube). Day 4–5: Design 3 samples. Day 6–7: Offer free designs to 2 businesses in exchange for testimonials.

Web Skills – Build & Sell Simple Sites

You already know HTML. Add basic CSS (1 week learning). Offer to build 1‑page websites for small shops at Ksh 3,000–10,000. Use free hosting + low‑cost domain.

Tools: HTML, CSS, Netlify (free hosting), Google Domains.

Yes. Many tasks—social media management, typing, Canva design, affiliate marketing—can be done entirely on a smartphone with internet.

If you follow the “Earn Now” steps actively, you could earn within 2–4 weeks. Selling notes or small design gigs are fastest.

Start with M‑Pesa, PayPal, Payoneer, or bank transfer. For international clients, PayPal is most common.
